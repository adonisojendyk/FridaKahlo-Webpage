Frida Kahlo

  • Born July 6, 1907, Coyoacán, Mexico
  • Contracted polio at age of 6 in 1913
  • Attended the National Preparatory School in Mexico city in 1922
  • 1925 Frida is severly injured on a bus and becomes unable to have children because of said injuries.
  • 1926 Frida makes a fake birth certificate of her imaginery son and starts taking painting seriously while in her hospital bed.
  • 1928 Frida Kahlo joins the Communist party and meets Diego Rivera
  • 1929 Diego and Frida marry and both leave the Communist Party.
  • Died July 13, 1954, Coyoacán, Mexico

"I am not sick. I am broken. But I am happy to be alive as long as I can paint."
- Frida Kahlo
